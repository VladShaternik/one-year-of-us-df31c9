PHOTOS GO IN THIS FOLDER
========================

Drop your images here using the filenames below. The site picks them up
automatically; until a file exists, that spot shows a botanical placeholder
with the filename written on it, so you can see exactly what is missing.

Tips:
- JPG is expected. If you use PNG/HEIC, either rename to .jpg or update the
  matching "photo:" path in the CONFIG block at the top of index.html.
- Resize to roughly 1200px on the long side so the page loads fast on a phone.
  (On a Mac: open in Preview, Tools > Adjust Size.)
- Story cards crop to 4:3 and polaroids crop to square, both centered. Faces
  near the middle of the frame work best.

Note: the two "born" cards (Vlad 1999, Macy 2004) do not use photos.
They play small animated scenes instead, since no baby pictures exist.
